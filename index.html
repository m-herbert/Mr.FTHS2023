Jack Goldsmith

I want to be Mr. FTHS because the reward of winning after all the hard work and early mornings we are about to put in will make it all worth it. I will enjoy the night regardless of the outcome, but winning would make the feeling much better. Also, both my brothers participated in it when they were here, and they couldn’t win, so a win under the Goldsmith name would be very nice.
